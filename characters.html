The "Friends" (Main Characters/Cast)

There are six main characters on the show Friends each with their own unique characteristics, fun personalities, catchphrases, and "interesting" backstories.

The Main Characters

  1. Ross Geller (portrayed by David Schwimmer)

    2. Ross is a paleontology professor at NYU. He is the older brother of Monica Geller. Ross had a crush on Monica's best friend Rachel Green for years, but has not told her. Eventually when he does tell her and they date, things go bad when Rachel suggests they "take a break" and Ross ends up sleeping with another woman. They break up and have a huge argument lasting years about it ("We were on a break!"). Ross has also been in three failed marriages (one with Rachel and they do get back together in the finale) including his lesbian ex-wife Carol, a British woman Emily, and as mentioned before...Rachel. They even have a kid together.

    Quotes

    • "We were on a break"
    • "Hi." (said awkwardly)
    • "I'm an 8!" (After messing up in a spray tan booth, twice, he gets four 2's on his front side and none on his back side)
  2. Rachel Green (portrayed by Jennifer Aniston)

    3. Rachel is an executive at Ralph Lauren that originally started off as that spoiled rich girl. She ran away from a wedding and came to Central Perk while still wearing the dress. After a huge argument with her dad (and her confusing him with a metaphor "she's a shoe"), she ends up cut off of her dad's money and must get a job. Eventually after working as a waitress at Central Perk, she becomes the executive at Ralph Lauren. She also had a bad relationship with Ross and had a kid with him (even though he was wearing a condom) and they get married twice...(once drunk in Vegas).

    Quotes

    • "Nooooooooo!" (when receiving bad news)
    • "Take a break" (from a relationship)
    • "Officer Handsome" (complementing a police officer to get out of getting a ticket)
  3. Chandler Bing (portrayed by Matthew Perry)

    4. Chandler is a junior advertising copywriter. He gets married to Monica eventually after they "do it" in London during her brother's second wedding. Ross has been his best friend since college and Chandler's former roommate Joey also holds that title. Chandler and Joey also adopted a chick and a duck which they treat like their children. In the earlier seasons, no one knew what he did for a job. Chandler also had a very awkward childhood with divorced parents and his dad becoming a woman (Helena Handbasket). Chandler also had the most annoying girlfriend in the world at one point, Janice who would always say "Oh My Gawd!".

    Quotes

    • "Can I/we be more...?" (varies when said (ex: "Can we be more white trash?" or "Can I be more sarcastic?"))
    • "And there's Daddy." (seeing his transgendered dad on stage at a transgendered restaurant)
    • "Oh My God!" (normally mocking Janice)
  4. Monica Geller (portrayed by Courteney Cox)

    5. Monica is the head chef at Javu. She is married to Chandler and is the best friend and former roommate of Rachel. She is the younger sister of Ross (who is the favorite of their parents). When she was younger, Monica was overweight.When she was called fat back then by Chandler, she got thin and tried to seduce him for revenge, however there was a freak accident with a knife and she cut Chandler's toe off. She also a one point had interest in Joey when he moved in, but she lost that when he thought she was trying to seduce him with the phrase "make you some lemonade"...or something like that. She also hated the downstairs neighbor Mr. Heckles, who was old and weird.

    Quotes

    • "I know!" (loud and high pitched)
    • "Rachel, what's your cat doing in my bowl" (when she sees a chicken defrosting and mistook it for Rachel's hairless cat)
    • "I used to hitch-hike their as a kid" (when Rachel brought up a bakery in New Jersey, "Corrino's")
  5. Joey Tribbiani (portrayed by Matt LeBlanc)

    6. Joey is a failing actor who lives in New York. He is the only male child out of his many siblings. He can be seen as that classic "guy who can always get the girls". He is best friends with Chandler and Ross. Joey is not that bright as he did not know you could eat birds, that "cupping" is only apart of how they make pants in prison, and he got tricked easily by a guy trying to grab his and Chandler's apartment. Joey is also very attached to his friends and does not want them to leave or for things to change. He even gets his own spin off Joey where he moves to Los Angeles. He also loves food as shown when he eats a whole turkey and gets fat.

    Quotes

    • "How you doin'?" (trying to pick up women or talking to the turkey he ate)
    • "Hi I'm Chandler, 'Can I be wearing any more clothes?'" (getting back at Chandler by wearing all of his clothes)
    • "It's 'Bunny vs. Doody'!" (at a Halloween party where Ross (Spudnik/"Doody" costume) is armwrestling Chandler (Bunny costume))
  6. Phoebe Buffay (portrayed by Lisa Kudrow)

    7. Phoebe is the orphaned singer/songwriter who primarily performs at Central Perk. Phoebe had a troubled childhood where her father abandoned her mother, she was left with her "fake mom" and the fake mom killed herself, she lived on the streets, a pimp spit in her mouth, and she also mugged Ross with a pipe. She's also a vegetarian and married to another character Mike Hannigan. Her primary song to sing at Central Perk is Smelly Cat. Phoebe can also be as stupid as Joey at times like when they were fake boxing, she hit him for real. Phoebe also has a twin sister Ursula who is from Mad About You and also has used Phoebe's name as a porn name.

    Quotes

    • "Oh no!" (when something bad happens)
    • "Regina Phalange" (an alias she used, alternatively was used one time used as an excuse to get Rachel Off a plane by saying she had a feeling "something's wrong with the left phalange")
    • "Now it's a party!" (after seeing the inappropriate cake that the bakery "Corrino's" mixed up with the bunny cake Rachel ordered)
