About Friends

Friends was an American sitcom about six friends living their lives in New York City, and hanging out at their favorite coffee place Central Perk and each others' apartments. With memorable characters, funny stories, and catchphrases galore, Friends is truly a classic show.

What Is This Site About?

This site will have content about the main characters and the setting of the TV show Friends and some major story arcs in the show, like Ross and Rachel's relationship. This site will also cover the funny and quirky personality traits and quotes of the main characters.
